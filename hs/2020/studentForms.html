Student Consent Forms

In order to participate in High School I/O, OHI/O's Code of Conduct & Photo Release must be filled out. Student participants must bring all required forms completed and signed to the event registration desk. Research participation is optional. At check-in, faculty will be present to assist with form collection and questions. Extra copies of the Code of Conduct will be available at the desk, however, please note that parental signatures are required.
