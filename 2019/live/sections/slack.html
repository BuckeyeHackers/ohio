Slack

This year we are using Slack as the platform for communication. It can be used through a web browser, web application and/or mobile app. You will have to create your own login to enter our slack workspace. In slack, there are dedicated channels for directed topics of conversation. Feel free to network and contact mentors if you are having any issues. Please use this link to join our workspace: https://go.osu.edu/hackohio2019slack

    Steps to sign up:
  1. Log-in with an email, and set up a password for your account. Please enter your name for your display name, and in your profile, please type in your role (i.e. Mentor/Student)
  2. You will automatically join the #general channel. Click on channels,
    here you may search for challenge you are working on or other relevant areas to direct your questions to.
  3. To join a channel, click on the channel name and then click the green 'Join Channel' button.
  4. Please direct your questions to mentors by utilizing the 'mentorship' channel or a channel related to your challenge.
  5. If you would like to reply to someone's message, hover or click on their message, and click ‘Start a Thread’.
  6. If you have any questions regarding use of slack, please feel free to contact one of the OHI/O team members!
