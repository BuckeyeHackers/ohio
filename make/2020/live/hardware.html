Hardware

Looking for Hardware? MakeOHI/O, in coordination with Electronics Club, is providing free hardware loans for students to use on their hacks. Hardware can be borrowed from the Help Desk at the bottom of the giant stairs. Below is just a subset of what we have!


Don't forget to take a valid ID with you.
